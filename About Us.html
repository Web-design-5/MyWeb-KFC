Welcome to KFC

We're a team of passionate individuals who are dedicated to providing finger-lickin' good food to our customers. KFC was founded in 1952 by Colonel Harland Sanders, and since then, we've grown to become one of the largest fast-food chains in the world.

Our History

KFC was founded in 1952 by Colonel Harland Sanders, who developed the secret recipe for our Original Recipe chicken. The first KFC restaurant was opened in Corbin, Kentucky, and the company quickly grew to become a national brand. Today, KFC is a global company with over 24,000 locations in more than 140 countries.

Our Values

We're guided by a set of core values that reflect our commitment to our customers, employees, and communities. These values include:

  • Customer focus: We're dedicated to serving our customers high-quality food that meets their needs and exceeds their expectations.
  • Integrity: We're committed to doing the right thing, even when it's difficult or unpopular.
  • Respect: We believe in treating our employees, customers, and partners with respect and dignity.
  • Teamwork: We're a team-oriented company that values collaboration and mutual support.
  • Accountability: We're accountable for our actions and our results, and we're committed to continuous improvement.
